We Have Answers

How does Focus AI work?

Focus AI analyzes your tasks, habits, and goals to create personalized schedules and recommendations. The platform continuously adapts to your workflow, helping you stay productive and focused.

Is Focus AI suitable for students?

Absolutely. Whether you're preparing for exams, managing assignments, or building new skills, Focus AI helps organize your workload and maximize your study time.

Can I customize my schedule?

Yes. Focus AI generates optimized schedules, but you always have full control to edit tasks, adjust priorities, and create a workflow that fits your needs.

How does distraction blocking work?

Focus AI identifies common distractions and helps you stay focused by creating dedicated work sessions, limiting interruptions, and encouraging deep work habits.

Does Focus AI learn from my behavior?

Yes. As you use the platform, Focus AI learns your productivity patterns, preferred working hours, and task completion habits to deliver smarter recommendations over time.

Can I use Focus AI on multiple devices?

Yes. Your tasks, schedules, and productivity data sync seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, so you can stay organized anywhere.
